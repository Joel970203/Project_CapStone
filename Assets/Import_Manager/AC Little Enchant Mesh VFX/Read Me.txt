Little Enchant Mesh VFX

Just drag and drop the prefab to use it (:
You just have to assign foreach particle system the mesh (in the shape section).
This pack contains 5 VFX with 3 levels (3 x 5 = 15).

For the demo scene, use 1920 x 1080 screen resolution.

To use this pack with URP, just double click on URPMaterials in the materials folder and export the new materials. 
You can also Upgrade the materials. 
To use this pack with HDRP, just double click on HDRPMaterials in the materials folder and export the new materials. 
You can also Upgrade the materials.
